Complete a Circle with us every Week.

Come aboard. Join an Network. Match up with exciting people. Set up a call. Get talking. Then do it all over again.


How does
it work?

Search for a Network

Send the request to join the network that compliments your needs. You will be granted access by ther network admin. Now you can use our Matching Tool.

Get Swiping

You can now look at profiles of different people there and start matching up with them. You will be granted 5 swipes a day! Use them wisely.

Matched Up? What next?

Select 3 timeslots from our predefined slots and send it over to your match. Your match can now finalize the slot and set the meeting.

Set up a call.

Once agreed by both parties, a zoom call link will be shared and an event will be created in your Google Calendar to remind you.
